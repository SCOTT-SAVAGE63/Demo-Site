Economic Justice & Job Creation

DEMO: Building an inclusive economy that works for all South Africans

Overview

Our economic policy framework focuses on reducing inequality, creating sustainable jobs, and building local manufacturing capacity to transform South Africa's economy.

Job Creation Initiative

DEMO: Create 2 million new jobs through infrastructure projects, green energy transition, and support for small businesses. Focus on youth employment in digital and creative sectors.

2M New Jobs Target
R50B Investment
5 Years Timeline

Small Business Support

DEMO: Establish a National Small Business Fund with R20 billion capitalization, simplified regulatory processes, and preferential procurement for locally-owned businesses.

Progressive Taxation

DEMO: Implement fair tax policies that ask corporations and high-income earners to contribute their fair share while providing relief to low-income households.

Land Reform & Housing Justice

DEMO: Equitable access to land and quality housing for all

Overview

Addressing historical land injustices while ensuring food security and providing adequate housing through sustainable development practices.

Accelerated Land Redistribution

DEMO: Transparent, market-based land reform with support for new farmers. Target: 30% of agricultural land under black ownership by 2030.

Current Progress
18% of 30% target

Affordable Housing Program

DEMO: Build 1 million affordable housing units in 10 years, with mixed-income developments and integrated urban planning.

Urban Renewal Initiative

DEMO: Transform townships and informal settlements through infrastructure upgrades, service delivery, and community ownership models.

Education Revolution

DEMO: Quality education from early childhood through tertiary

Overview

Transforming South Africa's education system to provide equal opportunities, modern skills, and prepare youth for the 21st century economy.

Universal Early Childhood Education

DEMO: Free, quality early childhood education for all children aged 3-5, with trained educators and modern facilities.

Digital Skills for All

DEMO: Integrate coding, digital literacy, and STEM education into school curriculum. Provide tablets and internet access to all students by 2025.

Free Tertiary Education Expansion

DEMO: Gradually expand free tertiary education to cover all qualifying students in critical fields like engineering, healthcare, and education.

Universal Healthcare Access

DEMO: Quality healthcare as a fundamental right

Overview

Building a comprehensive National Health Service that provides quality care to all South Africans, regardless of income or location.

National Health Service

DEMO: Phased implementation of universal healthcare coverage, starting with primary care and essential medicines.

Rural Health Infrastructure

DEMO: Build 200 new clinics and upgrade 500 existing facilities in rural and underserved areas over 5 years.

Mental Health Integration

DEMO: Integrate mental health services into primary care and establish community-based support networks nationwide.

Energy Security & Transition

DEMO: Ending load shedding, building renewable future

Overview

A comprehensive energy strategy that ends load shedding, transitions to renewable sources, and creates thousands of jobs in the green economy.

End Load Shedding

DEMO: Emergency investment in grid maintenance, independent power producers, and demand management to end load shedding within 18 months.

Target: End Load Shedding by
12 Months

Renewable Energy Expansion

DEMO: Achieve 60% renewable energy by 2035 through solar, wind, and green hydrogen investments, creating 500,000+ jobs.

Just Transition Framework

DEMO: Support for coal communities through retraining, economic diversification, and social protection during energy transition.

Environmental Stewardship

DEMO: Protecting South Africa's natural heritage

Overview

Balancing development with environmental protection, addressing climate change, and preserving South Africa's unique biodiversity for future generations.

Climate Action Plan

DEMO: Reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 45% by 2030 through renewable energy, transport electrification, and sustainable agriculture.

Water Security

DEMO: National water infrastructure investment, conservation programs, and addressing water pollution in mining-affected communities.

Biodiversity Protection

DEMO: Expand protected areas, combat wildlife trafficking, and promote sustainable tourism that benefits local communities.

Transparent Governance

DEMO: Accountable, transparent, and participatory government

Overview

Reforming governance systems to be more transparent, accountable, and responsive to citizen needs through digital innovation and participatory processes.

Anti-Corruption Commission

DEMO: Establish independent, well-resourced anti-corruption body with prosecutorial powers and protection for whistleblowers.

Digital Government

DEMO: Modernize public services through digital platforms, reducing bureaucracy and improving accessibility for all citizens.

Participatory Budgeting

DEMO: Citizens directly participate in allocating portion of municipal budgets through community meetings and digital platforms.
