Frequently Asked Questions

DEMO: Common questions about getting involved

Do I need political experience to volunteer?

No prior political experience is required. We provide training and support for all volunteers, regardless of background. Many of our most effective volunteers started with no political experience.

How much time do I need to commit?

We have opportunities for every schedule. You can volunteer as little as 1-2 hours per week or take on more substantial roles. We work with your availability and preferences.

Is there an age requirement to join?

You must be 16 or older to volunteer independently. Youth between 13-15 can volunteer with parental consent and supervision. There's no upper age limit!

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We have many digital volunteer opportunities including social media support, content creation, data entry, and virtual phone banking that can be done from anywhere.
